here you can find the data- metaanalysis for the data presented in the manuscript and SM.The data are categorized in ecogenions and the net and trap locations. 
The data in this folder are in csv or xlsx format. They can also be found in nc format in the model output folder. 



foraminifera_SEASONALITY_SIGMA2_PALATABILITY09			: foraminifera seasonal abundance 

foraminiferaBIOMASSONLY_SEASONALITY_SIGMA2_PALATABILITY09 	: foraminifera seasonal biomass

forams_seasonality__export_production_sigma2_palatability_09	: foraminifera seasonal export production

zooplankton_190um_SEASONALITY_SIGMA2_PALATABILITY09		: zooplankton 190um seasonal biomass

foraminifera_biomass_RCP6.xlxs					: foraminifera biomass from 1776 to 2100 under RCP6.0 emmissions 

foraminifera_biomass_RCP8p5.xlxs				: foraminifera biomass from 1776 to 2100 under RCP8.5 emmissions
